# Seasonal Arc Model

This document explains the repeating seasonal cycles that define the progression of the Vespera ARG.

Three pillars define each season:
1. The Long Shadow
2. The Ascent
3. The Event Horizon

------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. PILLAR I: THE LONG SHADOW (Slow Burn)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

__Purpose:__
- Introduce world, Veil, and minor breaches.

__Duration:__
- Flexible (weeks to months).

__Characteristics:__
- Daily minor breaches at 03:13.
- Early Veil artifacts.
- Environmental distortions (lights, clocks, reflections).
- No major confrontations.

__Tone:__
- Veil: clinical and detached.
- Vespera: distant, judging, rarely centered.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. PILLAR II: THE ASCENT (Seasonal Arc)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

__Purpose:__
- Escalate tension and deepen lore.

__Characteristics:__
- Major breach frequency increases.
- Vespera becomes more present and articulate.
- Veil degradation begins:
  - Memory errors
  - Missing hours
  - Terse logs

Community triggers:
- Hidden 0313 codes
- Spectrogram clues
- Multi-feed secrets

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. PILLAR III: THE EVENT HORIZON (Annual Collapse)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

__Purpose:__
- Deliver yearly climax without ending ARG.

__Characteristics:__
- Storm-class breach on 03/13 at 03:13 EST.
- Multi-feed grid rupture.
- Major revelations:
  - The Watch
  - Echo-Vesperas
  - Husband lore

__Outcome::__
- Rules for next season change.
- Possible Veil succession.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
4. ENGAGEMENT RESPONSIVENESS
------------------------------------------------------------------------

__Adaptive design:__
- Fast follower growth: 
  - Escalate sooner
  - Introduce deeper lore
- Slow growth:
  - Extend Long Shadow
  - Use subtle anomalies

------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. FUTUREP-PROOFING
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Future cycles may introduce:
- New Veils
- New entities
- New rituals
- Timeline wars
