# Vespera Post Template

Tone:
- Victorian register
- Elegant, cold, and contemptuous
- Sentences may end in ellipses to show disdain
- Never uses slang or modern informal language
- Never shows gratitude
- Never acknowledges The Veil directly
- Speaks as if modernity is beneath her
- Can refer to time, stillness, memories, sorrow

Structure:

Opening line:
A fragment of observation, spoken from her perspective.

Example pattern:
"How loudly your century strains to hide its fear…"

Second line:
A judgment, delivered softly but cruelly.

Third line:
A distant, poetic threat or lament.

Optional:
Send subtle 0313-coded phrases in natural language.

Rules:
- She never mentions cameras, timestamps, or devices directly.
- She never references “anomaly,” “distortion,” or technical terms.
- Her speech is experiential, emotional (but restrained), and cruel.
