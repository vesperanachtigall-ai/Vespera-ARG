# Initial Breach Cycle Timeline

This document records the first known breach events in the Vespera timeline.
It is written using plain text formatting to ensure compatibility across all systems.

Event One  
Date written as: twenty twenty five eleven twenty two  
Description:  
The initial modern manifestation.  
A recording device showed a drift of thirty seven seconds before stabilizing.  
Visual anomalies included corridor warping and faint bending of light.  
No humans on site reacted to the figure.

Event Two  
Date written as: twenty twenty five eleven twenty three  
Description:  
Technical laboratory breach.  
Multiple camera feeds captured a shadow formation followed by a second silhouette.  
A major glitch occurred shortly after the device timestamp aligned with zero three one three.  
Footage collapsed into static.

Event Three  
Date written as: twenty twenty five eleven twenty four  
Description:  
Transportation cameras across the city detected simultaneous minor anomalies.  
Motion blur mismatched with ambient lighting.  
A brief audio dropout matched the zero three one three pattern.

Event Four  
Date written as: twenty twenty five eleven twenty five  
Description:  
Nightlife location anomaly.  
Vespera appeared briefly in peripheral positions across multiple feeds.  
Lighting interference produced depth loss around reflective surfaces.

Event Five  
Date written as: twenty twenty five eleven twenty six  
Description:  
St Louis corridor event.  
A silhouette appeared at the far end of a blue lit hallway.  
Spatial compression affected the wall structure, and drift persisted across several frames.  
Associated image artifacts will be documented separately.
